Locations

The directory allows you to view the location of employee's desk, rooms and printers. Location filters allow you to focus on a particular individual or group to select from.

Employee Location

  • Selecting an employee from the list will display their desk location on a map of the office.
  • You may only select one employee at a time to view their desk location. Selecting an employee will deselect any selected employee.
  • When an employee location is displayed on the map, clicking on their name will take you to the profile of that employee.
  • When an employee location is displayed on the map, clicking on the map marker will remove the map marker and name from the map.
  • Clicking on other desks on the office map will display the employee that is assigned that desk location.

You can direct link to a particular desk by adding the desk location at the end of the employee location url

  • http://directory.liazon.corp/employees/map/buf-6/062405

Room Location

  • Selecting a room from the list will display its location on a map of the office.
  • You may only select one room at a time to view its location. Selecting a room will deselect any selected room.
  • When a room location is displayed on the map, clicking on the name of the room will take you to the profile of that room.
  • Clicking on the name of other rooms on the office map will take you to the profile of that room.

You can direct link to a particular room by adding the room name at the end of the room location url

  • http://directory.liazon.corp/rooms/map/buf-7/Allentown

Printer Location

  • Selecting a printer from the list will display its location on a map of the office.
  • On the map, color printers areblueand monochrome printers aregray
  • You may only select one printer at a time to view its location. Selecting a printer will deselect any selected printer.
  • When a printer location is displayed on the map, clicking on the name of the printer will take you to the profile of that printer.
  • Clicking on other printers on the office map will display the name of the selected printer.

You can direct link to a particular printer by adding the printer name at the end of the printer location url

  • http://directory.liazon.corp/printers/map/buf-7/LZB7P1-LEX510
